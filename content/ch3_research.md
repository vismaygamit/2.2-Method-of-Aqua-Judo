
## THE RESEARCH
**Why Judo Ages Well**
*The science behind the method*

Judo is one of the most effective training systems for aging bodies. This is not philosophy. It is measurable.

Scientists from Southern Cross University and the University of Sydney reviewed structured judo training programs in older adults and found consistent, significant improvements across every functional capacity that declines with age.

### WHY THESE RESULTS — A THREE-TIER VIEW
The evidence behind this method falls into three categories. Separating them is not a weakness. It is the difference between a method that knows what it knows and one that overstates everything equally.
