### WHY THESE MARKERS
* **Single-leg balance** is the most clinically validated predictor of fall risk in adults. It declines measurably with age and improves measurably with multi-planar balance training.
* **Uneven-surface walk** tests real-world proprioceptive function, not lab-controlled balance. It reflects what actually changes after coastal terrain training.
* **Breath-hold comfort** is a proxy for CO₂ tolerance and autonomic regulation—the physiological capacity to remain calm under internal pressure.
* **Recovery heart rate** reflects cardiovascular adaptability, not peak fitness. A faster recovery indicates a more resilient autonomic system.
* **Movement confidence** is self-reported and deliberately included. Perceived competence on unstable surfaces is both a training outcome and a fall-prevention factor in its own right.
 
These measurements are taken by the practitioner on Day 1 before any training, and on Day 7 after the synthesis session. The comparison is not a clinical trial. It is an honest record.
